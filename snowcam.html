Perisher

Carinya Village is conveniently located just 33 kilometers (20.5 miles) away from Perisher Ski Resort, making it an ideal base for winter adventures. Visitors can immerse themselves in a range of thrilling snow activities such as skiing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, and snow tubing. With picturesque trails, cross-country skiing options, and even snowmobile tours. Carinya Village provides easy access to an array of exciting activities at Perisher Ski Resort.

Thredbo

Carinya Village in Jindabyne is approximately 32 kilometers (20 miles) away from Thredbo Alpine Village, offering a convenient base for exploring the area. Visitors can indulge in a variety of activities, including skiing, snowboarding, and snowshoeing. With over 50 runs, ranging from gentle slopes to challenging terrain, there's something for every skill level. Carinya Village provides easy access to a range of activities at Thredbo.

Charlotte pass

Carinya Village in Jindabyne is approximately 50 kilometers (31 miles) away from Charlotte Pass, making it a convenient starting point for exploring this unique alpine village. At Charlotte Pass, visitors can enjoy a range of activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and snowshoeing. Whether you're a snow enthusiast or looking for a peaceful winter retreat, Carinya Village provides easy access to a variety of activities at Charlotte Pass.

Mount Selwyn

Carinya Village in Jindabyne is conveniently located approximately 73 kilometers (45 miles) away from Mount Selwyn, making it an excellent base for exploring the area. At Mount Selwyn, visitors can enjoy a range of winter activities such as skiing, snowboarding, and tobogganing. With gentle slopes and a family-friendly atmosphere, it's an ideal destination for beginners and families with children. Whether you're a seasoned snow enthusiast or new to winter sports, Carinya Village provides easy access to a variety of activities at Mount Selwyn.
